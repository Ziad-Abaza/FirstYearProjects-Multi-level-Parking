The Multi-Level Car Garage Project introduces an innovative solution for urban parking challenges. It features a modern, compact design that maximizes the number of parking spaces by utilizing vertical space efficiently. The garage is equipped with automated mechanisms to move vehicles between levels, reducing the time and effort needed for parking and retrieval. This project provides a safe, organized, and sustainable approach to managing increasing parking demands in urban environments.
